## English Transcript
**Narrator:**The forms that inhabits the woven textiles and the paper prints represents a crowd, a collective, or a network of people forming an interlacing connectivity. The work explores the meaning of the verb to the people which can suggest populating and inhabiting a space, either wilfully or through compulsion, but can also be read as a work that is for people referring to one person or multiple individuals.
